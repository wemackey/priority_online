Instructions

At the end of the sequence you will see a question mark in the center of the black dot.

If you saw a number (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, or 9) in the series of letters, press the "Y" key on your keyboard. If you did NOT see a number in the series of letters, press the "N" key on you keyboard.

To make answering easier, we recommend putting your left middle finger on the "Y" key, and left pointer finger on the "N" key.
