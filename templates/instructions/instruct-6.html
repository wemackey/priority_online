Instructions

Immediately after you answer either (Y)es or (N)o, a red dot with two arrows ( < O > ) will appear in the center of the black dot along with another red dot somewhere on the screen.

The red dot in the center of the screen asks you to determine whether the red dot on the screen currently is to the left or to the right of the red dot you had seen initially at the beginning of the trial.

If the dot that is currently on the screen is to the LEFT of the dot you had initially seen, press the LEFT arrow key on your keyboard (use your right pointer finger).

If the dot that is currently on the screen is to the RIGHT of the dot you had initially seen, press the RIGHT arrow key on your keyboard (use your right ring finger).
